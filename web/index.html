ABOUT QWIXPERT

Qwixpert is a marketplace which connects businesses with industry experts and independent consultants on a need basis for consulting assignments, advisory roles or to fill a short-term talent gap. Our vision is to the efficient, seamless, credible and the largest platform for client-expert engagement.

HOW IT WORKS

EXPLAIN YOUR NEEDS

Describe your needs to us. We will help you structure the problem. It ensures that the right experts are shortlisted.



CUSTOMIZED LIST OF TOP EXPERTS

Based on the problem structure, QWIXPERT will search our network of experts. We provide you with the experts who match your needs.



CHOOSE YOUR MATCH

Evaluate the list of profiles. Select an expert based on interviews/interactions, feedback from previous engagements, ratings, reviews, compliance and pricing.



ENGAGE YOUR EXPERT

Agree on milestones and deliverables. Start the engagement instantly. You can choose your expert on an/a:
a.Advisory assignment- Seeking a point of view or opinion or a solution through a telephonic call or video/voice chat through skype
b.Consulting assignment- Seeking expert solution along with analysis and simulation of business case and data.
c.Complex assignment- Seeking expert solution, analysis and implementation of the solution for a division or multiple divisions. If you require experts for such an assignment,Contact US. We shall help you assemble the team and deal the hassle of project management.



PAY ON DELIVERY

The fee for the engagement has to be paid to us when you select the expert. The money will be held safely in an escrow account and would be paid to the expert only on completion of milestones
At the end of the project, provide feedback about the expert.
